About me

I am a French engineer, PhD in Astronomy and Astrophysics, and freelance developer. Quite honest and competitive, you can mostly find me on basketball courts, behind my computer, crafting in my backyard, hanging out with my friends, or being in touch with my family.


Born in Nantes, in the west of France, where I spent my childhood until I went to the Supélec engineering school (now CentraleSupélec). I lived in London for a whole year, where I did a MSc in Applied Mathematics at Imperial College London. Next, I graduated from the Master Recherche in Astronomy and Astrophysics and Space Engineering at Université de Paris. I followed this path with a PhD in Astronomy and Astrophysics, specialising in the evolution and properties of galaxies.



I have been lucky enough to work in a variety of fields, from order picker in a warehouse to prototyping manager in a start-up, via international banking and research into Quantum Mechanics and Astrophysics.
